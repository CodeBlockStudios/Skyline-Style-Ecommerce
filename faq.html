Frequently Asked Questions

Is your site secure?

Yes. All credit card information is encrypted across a secure line.

Do I need to create an account to place an order?

Yes, an account is required to place an order with Skyline Style. With an account, you may save your billing and shipping addresses, join our mailing list, check the progress of your order, and build a wish list to share with friends! In addition, if there is a problem with your order, we will notify you.

What if I need to change my order after I place it?

We begin working on your order as soon as you place it. Unfortunately, we cannot make modifications to an order once it has been placed. We may be allowed to cancel orders if they have not shipped in certain circumstances.

How do I know if my order went through?

You should see an order confirmation screen, and will be emailed an order number with details of your order shortly after placing it.

Why haven't I received an email regarding my order?

There's typically two reasons for this: you may have an out-of-date email address on file, or you may have a spam filter that is filtering emails from us. Make sure your user information is up to date, and consider disabling any spam filters that may be preventing our mails from reaching you.

Can I back-order an item that is sold-out?

No. We have a high turnover of styles and must create room for new items. The good news is that really popular products can be reordered.

When will I receive my order?

All orders take 1-2 business days to process, regardless of the shipping method used. Please keep this in mind when selecting shipping options. Orders are shipped Monday through Friday. Skyline Style may need to contact you if there is an issue with your order, so please keep your account information updated! All order communication is done via email, so it is important that your email address be accurate and current.

Your order may be delayed because the Skyline Style warehouse is closed on the following days:

  • New Year's Day
  • Memorial Day
  • Labor Day
  • Thanksgiving Day
  • Christmas Day
