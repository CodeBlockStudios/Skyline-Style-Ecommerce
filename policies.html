Shipping & Return Policy

Shipping

Skyline Style ships to all 50 states, APO/FPO addresses, U.S. territories and possessions and all deliveries are subject to approval and availability of merchandise. Additionally, Skyline Style offers various shipping options to choose from, including standard and express delivery. The delivery time may vary depending on the shipping destination and availability of the merchandise. Please note that shipping and handling charges may apply and will be indicated during the checkout process. Skyline Style reserves the right to change its shipping policies at any time without prior notice.

All orders are processed and shipped during business hours Mondays through Fridays, as we are not operational on Saturdays, Sundays, or holidays. After you place your order, look for an email from us. We may require extra information in order to process your order.Customers can track their order by logging into their account or by contacting customer service. In case of any issues or delays, customers are requested to reach out to Skyline Style's customer service team, who will assist in resolving the matter.

Returns

We hope you love what you've ordered, but we know that sometimes things don't work out. Skyline Style accepts returns for items that are in new and unused condition, with the original tags and packaging, within 60 days of the purchase date.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect. In such cases, Skyline Style will provide a prepaid shipping label for the return. Refunds will be issued in the original form of payment. Exchanges can be made for a different size or color, subject to availability. Please allow for 1-2 weeks for processing time for returns.

Items that are not returnable include items not purchased from SkylineStyle.com (e.g., a third-party reseller, an auction site, etc.), items with removed tags or missing the original packaging, clearance items, washed/worn items, and jewelry.
